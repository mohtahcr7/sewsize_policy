Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025 · SewSize — Sewing Size Converter

1. Introduction

SewSize ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle any information when you use our mobile application SewSize (the "App").

2. Data Collection — Zero Data

SewSize does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data whatsoever. The App is designed as an offline-first tool with no internet connectivity. We do not operate servers, databases, or any backend infrastructure.

3. Local Storage Only

Any information you enter into the App (body measurements, profile names, conversion history, and app preferences) is stored exclusively on your device using local storage (Hive database). This data never leaves your device.

4. No Internet Access

SewSize does not request or use internet permission. The App functions completely offline. No data is ever transmitted to any external server, API, or third party.

5. Third-Party Services

SewSize does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or tracking SDKs. No Google Analytics, Firebase, AdMob, or similar services are used.

6. Data Sharing

Since no data is collected, no data is shared with any third party. We do not sell, trade, or transfer any information to outside parties.

7. Children's Privacy

SewSize is safe for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. As no data is collected at all, there is no risk of inadvertent collection.

8. User Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • Access: All your data is visible within the App screens.
  • Modify: You can edit any measurement or profile at any time.
  • Delete: You can delete individual profiles or clear all data from the Settings screen.
  • Uninstall: Uninstalling the App removes all locally stored data permanently.

9. Data Retention

Data remains on your device until you choose to delete it or uninstall the App. We have no ability to access, retain, or recover your data since it never reaches our systems.

10. Security

Since all data resides locally on your device, it is protected by your device's built-in security features (screen lock, encryption, etc.). We recommend using your device's security settings to protect your information.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted within the App. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
